Module 2 · Activity 1 of 3

AI Tools You Already Use

⏱ About 5 minutes

Before reading about the AI ecosystem, take stock of what you already use. Most people interact with AI tools every day without thinking of them as AI.

📱 Which AI tools have you used in the last month — even casually?
Think broadly: translation tools, writing assistants, image generators, chatbots on websites, recommendation systems, voice assistants…
🤔 Did you think of any of these as "AI" when you were using them?
💡 The point: The AI ecosystem is already part of daily life. Understanding where these tools sit in the stack helps you make smarter choices about which ones to adopt for your venture.
Module 2 · Activity 2 of 3

Stack Mapping

⏱ About 10 minutes

The AI stack has five layers — from physical hardware at the bottom to the apps you use at the top. For each layer, note which tools or players you're aware of, and what it means for your venture.

5
Applications — Workflows, Services, Apps
e.g. Canva, ChatGPT, HubSpot, Salesforce — this is where your venture operates
4
Deployment & Data Infrastructure
e.g. Snowflake, Palantir, Oracle — connects models to applications
3
Foundational Models & LLMs
e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta — the AI engines powering most tools
2
Data Centres & Cloud Infrastructure
e.g. AWS, Azure, Google Cloud — physical and cloud infrastructure
1
Compute — Chips & Hardware
e.g. NVIDIA, AMD — the physical foundation of AI
✅ Key insight: Opportunities for social ventures are at Layer 5 — the application layer. You don't need to build AI. You need to use it thoughtfully.
Module 2 · Activity 3 of 3

Reflection

⏱ About 5 minutes

The AI ecosystem raises questions about power, dependency, and data. These reflection questions help you think about what the stack means for your venture specifically.

🔒 Who controls the data your venture generates when using AI tools — and do you know whose infrastructure it runs on?
⚡ The AI stack has a significant environmental footprint. Does that change how you think about which tools to use?
🌍 The value chain ends in "business/mission impact." What impact would you most want AI to create for your venture in the next year?
