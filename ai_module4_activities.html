Module 4 · Activity 1 of 3

Warm-up — Where Do You Lose Time?

⏱ About 5 minutes

Level 1 AI is about saving time and increasing reach without changing your impact model. Before reading the tools list, identify where time is lost in your own work.

⏰ In a typical work week, which tasks take the most time but feel the most mechanical or repetitive?
Think about writing, communicating, translating, organising, reporting, analysing…
🌍 Where could your venture reach more people if you had more capacity or multilingual capability?
Module 4 · Activity 2 of 3

Level 1 Prompt Exercise

⏱ About 12 minutes

Use the Level 1 prompt framework from the module to brainstorm AI opportunities for your venture. Open a free AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — any will work) and use the prompt below, then record your best ideas here.

The prompt to use:

"As a social business focused on [your mission], brainstorm where AI could:
• Save Time — what repetitive tasks drain your team?
• Increase Reach — where could AI help you connect with more people without losing the human touch?

Consider: administration, communication, data, content.
Constraint: keep core mission unchanged and prioritise ethical tools.
Output: share your 3 most promising ideas and 1 concern to address."
💡 Your top 3 Level 1 AI ideas for your venture
⚠️ The one concern you need to address
What ethical, practical, or mission-related concern came up?
🛠️ Which of your top 3 ideas would you try first — and what is your next concrete step?
Module 4 · Activity 3 of 3

Level 2 Planning & Reflection

⏱ About 8 minutes

Level 2 AI is beneficiary-facing — it changes how people experience your service. Before going there, plan carefully and reflect on the ethical boundary between Level 1 and Level 2.

🤖 Is there a Level 2 use case that could meaningfully extend your reach or service quality?
e.g. a chatbot for FAQs, smart matching of participants, multilingual service access
❓ Before deploying any beneficiary-facing AI: "If this AI gives someone the wrong answer, what happens to them — and do we have a way to catch it?"
🔍 Reflection: What is your biggest concern about using AI — at Level 1 or Level 2 — in your venture's specific context?
