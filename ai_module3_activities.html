Don't think about what's realistic — just your honest first instinct

Module 3 · Activity 2 of 3

Integration Level Self-Assessment

⏱ About 8–10 minutes

The integration pyramid runs from T (Tool — using ready-made apps) to IP (Intellectual Property — building your own neural networks). For each level, reflect on where your venture currently is and where it could realistically go.

T — Tool
Using ready-made AI tools — no coding required
API — Integration
Connecting AI via APIs to automate workflows
AIP — Configurable AI
Customising AI models with your own data
PAI / IP — Proprietary or Custom AI
Building your own ML models or neural networks
📍 Where does your venture realistically sit right now — and where should it aim in 2 years?
Module 3 · Activity 3 of 3

Three Roles — Map Your Venture

⏱ About 10 minutes

Now apply the three roles framework directly to your venture. For each role, describe what AI at that level would look like — and whether it makes sense.

Supportive
Background — AI streamlines internal processes
Integral
Enhancing — AI adds value to services or products
Core
Essential — the venture depends entirely on AI
✅ Which role is right for your venture — and what is the shift you would need to make to move one level deeper?
Think about what would need to grow, change, or be added — in you, your team, or your environment

✓ Module 3 complete

You've assessed your integration level, mapped all three roles to your venture, and identified what shift would move you deeper.
